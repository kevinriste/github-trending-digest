No 06  ·  Product Plate  ·  Strategic Merger

Ggml.ai joins Hugging Face to ensure the long-term progress of Local AI

github.com  ·  836 pts  ·  225 comments  ·  discussion

Analysis

The team behind ggml.ai, the creators of the `llama.cpp` library, has announced they are joining Hugging Face to formalize their ongoing collaboration. The move is intended to provide the `ggml` project with long-term sustainable resources while ensuring its core libraries remain open-source and community-driven. Moving forward, the joint efforts will prioritize seamless integration between `llama.cpp` and the Hugging Face `transformers` library, as well as improvements to the packaging and user experience of local inference software.
