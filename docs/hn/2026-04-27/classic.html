By transforming high-dimensional vectors so that every coordinate follows a known, fixed density, the method eliminates the need for expensive per-block scale factors or calibration during inference. This approach combines MSE-optimal quantization with a residual correction step to ensure inner-product calculations remain accurate and unbiased.
