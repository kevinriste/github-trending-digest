Fincept-Corporation/FinceptTerminal ↗

Technical Analysis

FinceptTerminal is a sophisticated, open-source financial intelligence platform designed for high-performance, institutional-grade market analysis. Built as a native C++20 application using the Qt6 framework, the software eschews heavy web technologies like Electron to deliver a streamlined, single-binary experience. Its core functionality spans a vast range of tools, including multi-asset analytics, real-time trading across 16 major brokers, and a QuantLib suite for complex derivatives pricing. The platform also integrates an advanced AI ecosystem featuring 37 specialized agents and support for local and cloud-based LLMs. Furthermore, users can tap into over 100 data connectors—ranging from standard market feeds to alternative datasets like maritime tracking and social sentiment—to power their investment research and algorithmic trading pipelines.

This project is primarily intended for professional traders, quantitative analysts, and data-driven investors who require robust, low-latency tools for deep market exploration. It is gaining traction because it addresses the growing demand for local, high-performance financial software that avoids the resource constraints and privacy concerns of web-based terminals. By offering a comprehensive "all-in-one" environment that combines visual node-based workflows with serious quantitative modeling, it empowers power users to build custom trading strategies without being locked into proprietary ecosystem limitations. The platform’s unique combination of AI-driven automation, extensive broker support, and a transparent, open-source architecture positions it as a compelling alternative to expensive, closed-source legacy financial software.

Shield // Ambient Sensing

RuView Tracks Presence Through WiFi Signal Analysis

github.com  ·  49,149 stars  ·  824 stars today

By processing radio frequency disturbances, this system monitors human movement without cameras or cloud-based surveillance. It provides a robust, local-first solution for sensitive environments that demand complete privacy.

Read →Share →
[ Analysis + ]

ruvnet/RuView ↗

Technical Analysis

RuView is an innovative open-source platform that transforms standard WiFi signals into a sophisticated spatial intelligence system without the need for cameras or wearable sensors. By utilizing Channel State Information (CSI) captured from low-cost ESP32-S3 hardware, the system detects micro-disturbances in radio waves caused by human presence, breathing, and heart rate. It employs a multi-frequency mesh architecture and spiking neural networks to reconstruct real-time data, including 17-point pose estimation and environmental mapping. Technically, the project leverages signal processing techniques like Fresnel zone geometry and multipath modeling to extract actionable insights from radio scatter patterns. By operating entirely at the edge, the system maintains high performance and sub-millisecond latency while ensuring all measurements are cryptographically attested for security.

This project is highly attractive to developers and organizations seeking privacy-compliant monitoring solutions that bypass the regulatory hurdles associated with video surveillance and health imaging. It holds significant potential for smart home automation, eldercare facilities, and retail analytics, where passive, non-intrusive monitoring of occupancy and vital signs is essential. The platform is currently trending because it offers a cost-effective, "physics-only" alternative to traditional sensor suites, allowing users to build advanced sensing networks for as little as $9 per node. By democratizing access to complex signal processing and spatial AI, RuView enables researchers and privacy-conscious builders to implement sophisticated tracking in environments where traditional cameras are either impractical or restricted.
